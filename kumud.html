Kumud Srinivasan

NAME Kumud Srinivasan
Nationality Indian
Occupation President & Vice President, Technology & Manufacturing Group, Intel India
Responsibilities Kumud Srinivasan is vice president of the Technology and Manufacturing Group, president of Intel India and general manager of the Intel Architecture Group, India.
In this capacity, she is responsible for general management of Intel's operations in India, which includes location's overall strategy, business-enabling operations, organizational development, engineering and innovation for market development, as well as managing relationships with government, industry and academia.

1981

Srinivasan received her bachelor's degree in economics from Calcutta University in 1981.

1984

She did her Master's degree in information and library studies from Syracuse University in 1984.

1987

She has completed doctoral coursework in information science at University of California, Berkeley.

1987

Srinivasan joined Intel in 1987 and has held several business and information systems positions within Intel's manufacturing and IT organizations.
