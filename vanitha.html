Vanitha Narayanan

NAME Vanitha Narayanan
Nationality Indian
Designation She is managing director of the IBM-Verizon relationship.
Education Narayanan has a Masters in Business Administration in Marketing from the University of Madras and a Masters in Business Administration in Information Systems from University of Houston - Magna Cum Laude.
Besides, she has a major in liberal arts from Stella Maris College, Chennai.

1987

Narayanan joined IBM in the US in 1987

2015

Narayanan was chairperson of the American Chamber of Commerce in India from 2015 to 2016

2017

Awards

In 2017 Narayanan was named as one of the ‘Most Powerful Women’ by Fortune India and Business Today.
She has been mentioned as one of the 12 Global Indian Super Women of 2016 by CXO Today.
